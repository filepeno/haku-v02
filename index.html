Features

More relevant search results

Our search algorithm finds results based on content - this means that it takes in consideration eg. linguistic factors such as grammar, actuality, content type, and how “deep” it lies on the website.

Use of machine learning and AI

Haku can be configured to use machine learning and AI. Advanced features include relevancy by behaviour, search on images based on similarity, and search on text based on meaning.

Our focus is to provide clients with an superior search experience no matter what language their website is on. Therefore Haku can be configured to support grammar in any language, and the search can be improved even further with additional synonyms.

Haku has a built-in support for Danish and English languages.
